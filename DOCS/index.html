Grant Origin


In January of 2021, the Tesla group, under the leadership of visionary entrepreneur Elon Musk, made a strategic decision to invest a substantial sum of $1.5 billion dollars in the cryptocurrency, Bitcoin. Unfortunately, the ensuing market downturn resulted in significant financial losses for many investors, including some who were dependent on these investments as a primary source of income. Tragically, some individuals even lost their homes due to the sharp drop in the value of the cryptocurrency market. In light of these events, Tesla and Elon Musk have made a magnanimous effort to support those who have suffered these losses. They have pledged to distribute $100,000,000 worth of crypto to those in need, with the aim of assisting as many affected individuals as possible in regaining their financial footing. This generous gesture reflects Tesla's and Musk's commitment to supporting their community and promoting economic recovery in the face of adversity.

Requirements check

To be considered for inclusion in this grant program, applicants must meet certain requirements that have been put in place to ensure that only qualified individuals participate in the selection process. These requirements are designed to ensure that the most deserving candidates are selected for the grant award.

Bitcoin Wallet Owners

When utilizing our blockchain platform, it is imperative that you provide a valid Bitcoin address to ensure that any grant rewards for which you qualify can be securely and accurately transferred. Please note that only individuals who hold a valid Bitcoin address will be eligible for these rewards.

Verified Email Address

During the registration process, it is crucial to provide a valid and active email address that can receive emails. Verification of this email address is essential to ensure that we can contact grant recipients and prevent any issues such as email bounces. Additionally, this step helps to maintain our organization's reputation for reliability and professionalism.


Blockchain connection

It is imperative that you register on platforms that support a shared, immutable ledger. This will facilitate the process of recording transactions and tracking assets within a business network. By using platforms that support this type of ledger, you can ensure that all transactions are transparent, secure, and tamper-proof.


18+ Age

The customary age at which individuals attain legal adulthood is 18, although the definition of adulthood may vary depending on legal rights, psychological development, and the country of residence. Typically, the desired age for grant applicants is 18 years or older.
